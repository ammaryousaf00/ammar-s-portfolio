Data analysis

In this mini project, I performed data analysis using Microsoft Excel to organize, clean, and interpret business-related datasets. I used formulas, pivot tables, and charts to summarize key insights such as sales performance, expenses, and growth trends. The project focused on improving decision-making by visualizing patterns through graphs and dashboards. This project enhanced my skills in data handling, visualization, and analytical thinking, and helped me understand how raw data can be converted into meaningful business insights.
