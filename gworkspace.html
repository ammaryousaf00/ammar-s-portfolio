Google Workspace

In this mini project, I used Google Workspace tools (Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Forms) to collaborate and manage tasks efficiently. The project involved creating shared documents for teamwork, designing Google Forms to collect responses, and analyzing the results in Google Sheets using formulas and charts. I also prepared a presentation in Google Slides to summarize findings and share insights with the team. This project improved my skills in online collaboration, data management, and cloud-based productivity tools.
